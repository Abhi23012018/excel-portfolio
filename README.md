# 🛍️ Vrinda Store — Annual Sales Analysis (Excel)

## 📌 Objective
Analyze Vrinda Store's 2022 annual sales data to uncover trends in revenue, customer demographics, top-performing regions, and sales channels — enabling data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel**
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Visualizations
- VLOOKUP / XLOOKUP
- Conditional Formatting
- Data Slicers & Filters

---

## 📂 Dataset
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Source | Vrinda Store Internal Sales Data |
| Year | 2022 (Full Year) |
| Total Records | 31,047 orders |
| Total Revenue | ₹2,11,76,377 |
| Columns | Order ID, Customer ID, Gender, Age, Date, Month, Status, Channel, Category, Size, Amount, Ship State, and more |

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🗓️ Monthly Performance
- **Highest sales in March** — ₹19,28,066 in revenue, making it the peak month of the year
- Sales remained strong from January through May before gradually declining in Q3–Q4
- November and December recorded the lowest revenue — suggesting an opportunity for festive-season campaigns

### 👥 Customer Demographics
- **Women drove 64% of total revenue** vs. Men at 36%
- **Adult age group** was the highest spending segment, followed by Teenagers and Seniors
- Targeting women aged 25–45 presents the strongest growth opportunity

### 🗺️ Regional Performance
- **Maharashtra performed best** with the highest state-level revenue
- Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh ranked 2nd and 3rd respectively
- Top 5 states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu

### 🛒 Sales Channels
- **Amazon was the #1 channel**, contributing the largest share of orders
- Myntra and Flipkart followed as strong secondary channels
- Ajio, Meesho, Nalli, and Others had smaller but notable contributions
- Multi-channel strategy is working — no single platform monopolizes sales

### 🏷️ Product Categories
- **"Set" category dominated** with the highest revenue across all product types
- Kurtas were the 2nd best-selling category
- Western Dress ranked 3rd, showing demand for non-traditional styles

### 📦 Order Fulfilment
- **92.3% of orders were successfully delivered**
- Returns, Cancellations, and Refunds combined account for under 8% — a healthy fulfilment rate
